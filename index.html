About

I am a biologist with extensive experience in GIS, remote sensing, and environmental investigation. I enjoy transforming raw spatial and ecological data into actionable insights for sustainable development—whether that's modeling urbanization effects on wildlife, monitoring forest cover with UAVs and satellite imagery, or designing geospatial workflows that keep decision-makers up to date in real time.
