Project Overview

I convened environmental experts, academic researchers, and local NGOs to collaboratively interpret landscape change along the Río Santa Catarina. By combining historical NDVI time series with community-driven cartography, the project builds a shared narrative of ecological degradation and recovery priorities.

The goal is to translate satellite-based vegetation trends into accessible maps that can guide restoration, policy, and public engagement for the river corridor.
